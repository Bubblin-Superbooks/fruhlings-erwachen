Ernest.

Let us skim!

Hans.

What remains the hens will eat.——I have pulled my head out of so many traps already——

Ernest.

Let us skim, Hans!——Why do you laugh?

Hans.

Are you beginning again already?

Ernest.

But one of us must begin.

Hans.
