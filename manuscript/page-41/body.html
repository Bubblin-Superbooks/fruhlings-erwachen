In hot weather, the boys as well as the girls, should wear nothing all day long but a short white woolen tunic with a girdle.——It seems to me that if they grew up that way they would be easier in mind than we are under the present regulations.

Melchior.

I believe so decidedly, Moritz!——The only question is, suppose the girls have children, what then?

Moritz.

How could they have children?
