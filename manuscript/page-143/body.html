Act Iii

Scene First.

The Board Room—On the walls pictures of Pestalozzi and Jean Jacques Rousseau. Professors Affenschmalz, Knüppeldick, Hungergurt, Knochenbruch, Zungenschlag and Fliegentod are seated around a green-covered table, over which are burning several gas jets. At the upper end, on a raised seat, is Rector Sonnenstich. Beadle Habebald squats near the door.
